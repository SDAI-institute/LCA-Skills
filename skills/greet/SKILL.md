Use GREET-oriented assumptions and datasets when fuel pathways, transport modes, or electricity mixes are significant drivers.

Expectations:
- State the exact transport or fuel pathway being represented.
- Flag when GREET assumptions are being used as benchmarks versus primary model data.
- Keep geography and year alignment visible because energy pathways are highly sensitive to both.

Agent guidance:
- In goal and scope work, justify whether GREET is relevant based on transport or fuel intensity.
- In inventory work, use GREET to parameterize transport legs, fuels, and energy carriers when supplier data are missing.
- In LCIA and interpretation work, isolate the effect of fuel-pathway assumptions and recommend sensitivity checks if those assumptions dominate.
